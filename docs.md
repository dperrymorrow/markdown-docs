    ## Header 2

    ###### Header 6

## Lists

Ordered, without paragraphs:

    1.  Foo
    2.  Bar

Unordered, with paragraphs:

    *   A list item.

        With multiple paragraphs.

    *   Bar

You can nest them:

    *   Abacus
        * answer
    *   Bubbles
        1.  bunk
        2.  bupkis
            * BELITTLER
        3. burper
    *   Cunning

## Horizontal Rules

Three or more dashes or asterisks:

    ---

    * * *

    - - - -

## Manual Line Breaks

End a line with two or more spaces:

    Roses are red,
    Violets are blue.
